In a country like Bangladesh where the job market is very fragile, where 47% of the educated population is unemployed, not enough jobs are created, there is a respectable position as an alternative profession, freelancing or freelance work, or more.

Broadly speaking, online profession. Freelancing is one of the many online professions.

Bangladesh is in a very good position in the freelancing sector, hundreds of crores of rupees are being spent on working with skilled professionals in the world. It is estimated that there are more than 5 lakh freelancers in Bangladesh.
Like freelancing, there are a lot of misconceptions about it, and by capitalizing on misconceptions, the trap of cheating unscrupulous traders has been created. All this will be discussed in this article.

Freelancing is to work on a project basis without having a permanent contract with any organization. People have been doing freelancing for hundreds of years. Like a rickshaw puller is also a freelancer, because he drives another's rickshaw, takes a passenger if he wants to, otherwise he doesn't. He has freedom. Nowadays, photographers are also freelancers, because they don't work as photographers anywhere, but shoot on a show basis and get paid.

So freelancing is not a job, just a way of working.

Online freelancing means that you will be contracted to do the work that the client will give you online, you will do the work with your own skills, and you will deliver it to the client online, and the client will pay you online. Didn't work online here, worked with your skills, just the medium online, the medium of communication. Just like if there is a work deal on the phone, surely you don't say make it on the phone, just like it is not online income.

Now with an example let's explain what is freelancer, freelancing, outsourcer, outsourcing.

Suppose you do good accounting. One of the American clients has to audit the company's account for 15 days. He offered on an online platform. Like many you applied. You got the job. Agreed on an amount payment. You did it with your skills, you delivered it online, the client paid you online.

Then

  • Who did the freelance work? You!

  • Who is a freelancer? You!

  • Who did the freelancing? You!

  • Who outsourced? Client!

  • Who is the outsourcer? Client

  • Who did the outsourcing? Client!

That means you are never outsourcing. The client is doing that! If someone claims he will teach outsourcing, consider him a fraud, because he himself does not know what outsourcing is. He is cheating. Since freelancing is not a job, if someone claims to teach freelancing, then he is also a fraud.
